🔍 What is a Bloch Sphere?

The Bloch Sphere is a 3D geometric representation of all possible quantum states of a qubit. Unlike classical bits that can only be 0 or 1, qubits exist as points on this sphere's surface, allowing infinite possibilities between |0⟩ and |1⟩ states through superposition.

🎯 What is Superposition?

Superposition is quantum mechanics' most mind-bending feature. While classical objects exist in one definite state, quantum objects can exist in multiple states simultaneously until measured. Think of it as nature's way of exploring all possibilities at once!

🪙
Classical Coin
Either heads OR tails
VS
🪙
Quantum "Coin"
Heads AND tails simultaneously!
|ψ⟩ = α|0⟩ + β|1⟩

Where: |α|² + |β|² = 1 (probability conservation)
α and β are complex numbers (amplitude + phase)
